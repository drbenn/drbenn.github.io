Personal Finance Dashboard 2021

Link to Google Sheets Example 2020 Personal Finance Dashboard
Link to Google Sheets 2021 Personal Finance Dashboard Blank Template

It's almost a new year, so time to update the personal financial tracker to ramble through another year. Minor updates to format and color scheme for something a bit fresher to look at in the month tabs. Most of the revamp efforts this year were in the form of the 'Dashboard' tab, providing an overarching summary that can be filtered in different manners, which has some features broken down by month, all the way to full-on long term time to retirement asset wealth.

Dashboard filters referenced in image above :
1. Filter Month for income and expenses, can view by single month or aggregate months together.

2. Filter Month for investment account balances, can view by single month or aggregate months together.

3. Stock month filter which shows detail of stock/funds inputted in seperate month tabs. Displays single month data only.

4. Invest Style slicer. Filters the stock/funds month detailed to its specified investment style (buy & hold, dividend, or speculation).
